Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the cheapest meal on the Panda Express menus?

The cheapest meal on the Panda Express menus is the Bowl starting around $9.30. The most affordable combination is Super Greens with Broccoli Beef. This gives you a filling meal for the lowest price.

2. Does the Panda Express menus have gluten free options?

Panda Express does not offer certified gluten free options. Almost all entrees contain soy sauce which is brewed with wheat. Even plain rice is prepared in a kitchen that handles wheat products. People with celiac disease should be very careful.

3. Can I order half and half sides on the Panda Express menus?

Yes you can ask for half and half sides at no extra charge. For example you can get half chow mein and half fried rice in one serving. This is a great way to get variety without paying more.

4. What is the highest protein item on the Panda Express menus?

The highest protein item on the Panda Express menus is the Grilled Teriyaki Chicken with 33 grams of protein per serving. For a full meal, order a Plate with Super Greens, Grilled Teriyaki Chicken, and Black Pepper Sirloin Steak. This gives you over 55 grams of protein.

5. How do the Panda Express menus prices compare to 2020?

Panda Express menus prices have increased since 2020. A Bowl cost $7.60 in 2020 and now costs $9.30. A Plate cost $9.50 in 2020 and now costs $11.10. The increases are about 20 percent over five years.
